Mark Borman

Film Photographer — Lisbon, Portugal

Freelance product and editorial photographer specializing in analog/film photography. Based in Lisbon, Portugal.

I shoot on a Mamiya RB67 and Contax T2, primarily on Kodak Portra 400. I work with small brands — ceramics studios, coffee roasters, leather craftsmen — to create warm, textured imagery that feels timeless.

Previously three years at a creative agency in Portland, Oregon. Visual Communication, Portland State University (2013–2017).
